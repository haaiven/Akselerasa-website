# PT IJA / AKSELERASA Landing Website - Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
**Business Card-Inspired Theme**: The website design is based on the official AKSELERASA business cards - warm cream backgrounds, golden amber accents, and maroon/dark brown text for a professional yet friendly street food aesthetic.

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetic**: Clean, minimalist, cafe-inspired with hand-drawn illustration feeling
- **Color Palette**: 
  - **Background**: Warm cream/off-white (#F5F2ED)
  - **Primary**: Golden amber/orange (HSL 38, 90%, 52%) - matching the logo
  - **Accent**: Maroon/dark red (HSL 0, 55%, 35%) - for important text highlights
  - **Text**: Dark brown (HSL 15, 45%, 22%) - professional and readable
  - **Muted**: Warm gray tones for secondary text
- **White Space**: Generous, full-width sections with breathing room
- **Brand Personality**: Warm, friendly, approachable, cafe-style yet credible and professional

## Typography
- **Brand Name "AKSELERASA"**: Cinzel font for elegant branding
- **Headings**: Large, bold typography in dark brown/maroon tones
- **Body Text**: Clean, highly readable in warm gray/brown tones
- **Hierarchy**: Strong contrast between heading sizes and body text

## Logo
- **Primary Logo**: Cart + Circuit icon (gerobak + teknologi) in golden amber
- **Represents**: Street food tradition meets digital technology innovation

## Layout System
- **Spacing Units**: Use Tailwind spacing of 4, 8, 12, 16, 20, and 32
- **Container**: Full-width sections with max-w-7xl inner containers
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first approach, stacking columns on mobile

## Component Library

### Navigation (Sticky Header)
- Logo on left (cart-circuit icon + AKSELERASA text in Cinzel)
- Menu items on right: What We Offer, Our Clients, Contact
- Smooth scroll behavior to sections
- Clean, minimal navigation bar with cream background

### Hero Section
- Full-width background image (street food scene)
- Large headline with clear value proposition
- Dual CTAs: Primary amber button + Secondary outline button
- Professional yet warm appearance

### Content Sections
- **What We Offer**: 4 service cards (UMKM Empowerment, POS System, Operational, Data Driven)
- **Our Clients**: Portfolio showcase with Holland Mini Café and Pukis Kota Baru
- **Contact Section**: 
  - WhatsApp button (primary action)
  - Phone and Email side-by-side
  - Contact form for messages
  - Newsletter signup

### Footer
- Logo and copyright
- Contact information
- Floating WhatsApp button with orbiting cart animation
